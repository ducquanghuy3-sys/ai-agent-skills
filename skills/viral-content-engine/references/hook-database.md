## Twitter/X Hooks

### Bold Claims
1. "Hot take: [opinion]. And I have data to prove it."
2. "Stop saying [common belief]. Here's what's actually true:"
3. "Unpopular opinion: [X] is massively overrated. Here's why:"
4. "[X] is dead. Here's what's replacing it."
5. "Everyone is talking about [X]. Almost everyone is wrong."

### Thread Openers
6. "I analyzed [X] [things] in [Y] days. Here are [N] lessons that changed how I think:"
7. "Nobody talks about this, but [topic] is about to [change/explode/collapse]."
8. "The difference between [A] and [B] is simpler than you think. A thread 🧵"
9. "In [year], I [achievement/failure]. This is the story nobody asked for:"
10. "I spent $[X] learning [topic] so you don't have to. Everything I know in one thread:"

### Data/Insight
11. "[Shocking statistic]. Most people have no idea."
12. "I tracked [X] for [time period]. Here's what I found:"
13. "The [industry] just hit an inflection point. Let me show you."
14. "Here's a chart that should scare/excite you: [describe]"
15. "[Company/Person] just did [X]. Here's why it matters more than you think."

### Engagement Bait (Ethical)
16. "What's the most underrated [topic] nobody talks about? I'll start:"
17. "Describe [topic] in 3 words. Wrong answers only."
18. "The best [topic] advice I ever got was [X]. What's yours?"
19. "Reply with your [X] and I'll [offer value in return]."
20. "This will be controversial but [reasonable take framed provocatively]."

## LinkedIn Hooks

### Career Story
1. "I got rejected from [X] jobs. Then I changed one thing on my resume."
2. "3 years ago I was [bad situation]. Today I [good outcome]. Here's the turning point:"
3. "My worst manager taught me my best lesson."
4. "I almost quit my job last month. Glad I didn't. Here's what happened."
5. "The smartest career move I made had nothing to do with my career."

### Industry Insight
6. "[Industry] is going through its biggest shift in [X] years. Most people don't see it yet."
7. "After [X] years in [field], I've noticed a pattern that nobody talks about."
8. "The companies winning right now all have one thing in common."
9. "I reviewed [X] [resumes/proposals/strategies] this month. The #1 mistake:"
10. "The future of [industry] looks nothing like what the experts predicted."

### Framework/Lesson
11. "The 3 skills that separate good [role] from great ones:"
12. "Everything I know about [topic] fits on one page."
13. "If I had to start my career over, I would do these [N] things differently."
14. "The [role] playbook is broken. Here's what works in 2026:"
15. "I boiled [X years] of [field] experience down to [N] rules:"

### Counter-Narrative

18. "The worst career advice I keep hearing: [common advice]."

20. "We need to talk about [uncomfortable industry topic]."
